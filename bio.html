About Me:

The last name is pronounced the same as "dough" or "faux". Yes, my initials do spell "elf". No, I am not at all embarrassed by it. Some of my favorite people call me "elf".

And yes, the web address is, therefore, a play on "faux fur" only it is "fough fibre" because I am a spinning hobbiest with about 20+ pounds of washed but unprocessed sheep's wool in her spare room.

I am a native Oregonian who has also lived in Chicago, New York, and Denver.

I finished bootcamp at PDX Code Guild in June 2016. I have experience working in Python, Javascript, HTML5, CSS, jQuery, and Django.
